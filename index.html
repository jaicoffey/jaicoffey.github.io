2. Roald Dahl's different careers

Posted on 22nd March 2021

Roald Dahl has had many different careers, such as a pilot, company worker and an author. Read on to find out more about the different careers of Roald Dahl.

During the Second World War Dahl was a fighter pilot – then a spy. After leaving school in 1934 at the age of 17, Roald Dahl worked for the Shell Oil Company. Following the outbreak of the Second World War in 1939, Dahl joined the Royal Air Force (RAF) and for a period piloted a Hawker Hurricane fighter, and then he finally became a childrens' author.
